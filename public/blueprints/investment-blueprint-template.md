## CAPITAL ALLOCATION FRAMEWORK

Use this template to allocate your business funding across
asset classes for maximum growth and protection.

---

## TIER 1: EMERGENCY RESERVE (20% of Capital)

| Allocation | Vehicle | Expected Return | Liquidity |
|-----------|---------|----------------|-----------|
| 10% | High-Yield Savings Account | 4-5% APY | Immediate |
| 10% | Money Market Fund | 4-5% APY | 1-2 days |

**Target Amount:** 6 months of personal + business expenses
**Priority:** MUST be funded before investing elsewhere

---

## TIER 2: BUSINESS REINVESTMENT (40% of Capital)

| Allocation | Category | ROI Target | Timeline |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|
| 15% | Marketing & Ads | 3-5x ROAS | Monthly |
| 10% | Team / Contractors | 2-4x revenue multiplier | Quarterly |
| 10% | Tools & Systems | Time savings = $ value | Monthly |
| 5% | Education & Skills | Lifetime ROI | Ongoing |

**Rule:** Every dollar reinvested should produce at least $2 in revenue.

---

## TIER 3: WEALTH BUILDING (30% of Capital)

| Allocation | Vehicle | Expected Return | Timeline |
|-----------|---------|----------------|----------|
| 15% | S&P 500 Index Fund (VOO/SPY) | 8-12% avg/yr | 5+ years |
| 10% | Real Estate (REITs or Direct) | 6-10% + equity | 3-10 years |
| 5% | Alternative Assets (Crypto, Private Deals) | Variable | Variable |

**Index Fund Strategy:**

- Dollar-cost average every month
- Never panic sell
- Reinvest all dividends
- Time horizon: minimum 5 years

**Real Estate Entry Points:**

1. REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts) — start with $500
2. House Hack — live in one unit, rent the others
3. BRRRR Method — Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat

---

## TIER 4: ACCELERATION (10% of Capital)

| Allocation | Category | Risk Level | Upside |
|-----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| 5% | High-growth opportunities | High | 10x+ |
| 5% | Strategic partnerships/JVs | Medium | 3-5x |

**Rules for Tier 4:**

- Only money you can afford to lose completely
- Never borrow to invest in Tier 4
- Due diligence: minimum 30 days research before committing
