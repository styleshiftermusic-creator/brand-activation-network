## CHANNEL 1: PODCAST GUESTING

### The 5-Minute Pitch Template

**Subject:** Love your episode on [specific topic] — quick collab idea

Hi [Host Name],

I just listened to your episode on [specific topic] and loved how you broke down [specific point they made]. Your audience clearly cares about [their niche].

I help [your target market] achieve [specific result] using [your unique method]. One of my recent clients [brief result — e.g., "went from $0 to $150K in business funding in 90 days"].

I think your listeners would love a deep dive on [proposed topic that serves their audience].

Would you be open to a 30-minute conversation?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Website]

## CHANNEL 2: SHOUT-OUT PAGE STRATEGY

### Finding Shout-Out Pages

1. Search Instagram: "shout out page" + [your niche]
2. Look for pages with 10K-100K followers (sweet spot)
3. Check engagement rate (>3% is good)
4. DM or email for rates

### Shout-Out Post Template

**Hook:** "How [person] went from [before state] to [after state] in [timeframe]"

**Body:**
"Most people think [common misconception].

But [Name] discovered that [your framework/method] is the real key.

In just [timeframe], they:
✅ [Result 1]
✅ [Result 2]  
✅ [Result 3]

Want the exact blueprint? Link in bio → [your offer]"

## CHANNEL 3: CONTENT FACTORY WORKFLOW

### The 1-to-10 Content System

Start with ONE piece of long-form content per week:

```
1 Podcast Episode or Live Stream (30-60 min)
     ↓
  3 YouTube Clips (5-10 min each)
     ↓
  5 Instagram Reels / TikToks (15-60 sec)
     ↓
 10 Twitter/X Posts (text excerpts)
     ↓
  2 LinkedIn Articles (repurposed)
     ↓
  1 Email Newsletter (weekly digest)
```

### Weekly Content Calendar

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Time |
|-----|----------|-------------|------|
| Monday | YouTube | Full episode upload | 10am |
| Tuesday | Instagram | Reel #1 + Carousel | 12pm |
| Wednesday | Twitter/X | Thread from episode | 9am |
| Thursday | Instagram | Reel #2 + Story | 12pm |
| Friday | LinkedIn | Article + Newsletter | 8am |

## CHANNEL 4: JOINT VENTURE PARTNERSHIPS

### JV Email Template

Subject: Partnership idea — [specific opportunity]

Hi [Name],

I noticed you serve [their audience type] and I serve [your audience type].
There's zero competition between us, but our audiences would LOVE each other.

Here's what I'm thinking:

**Option A:** I promote your [product/service] to my list, you promote mine.
**Option B:** We co-create a [webinar/workshop/challenge] and split leads.
**Option C:** You send traffic to my funnel, I pay you [X%] commission.

I've got [your audience size/metric] and my audience converts at [conversion rate].

Worth a 15-minute call to explore?

[Your Name]
