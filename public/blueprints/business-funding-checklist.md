# BUSINESS FUNDING CHECKLIST

## Brand Activation Network — Blueprint BP-02

---

## PHASE 1: CREDIT FOUNDATION (Weeks 1-4)

- [ ] Check all 3 credit bureaus (Experian, Equifax, TransUnion)
- [ ] Dispute any inaccurate negative items
- [ ] Pay down credit utilization below 30%
- [ ] Open 2-3 pledge loans at different credit unions
- [ ] Set up autopay on ALL existing accounts
- [ ] Get added as authorized user on high-limit card (if possible)
- [ ] Target credit score: 680+ minimum, 720+ preferred

---

## PHASE 2: BUSINESS ENTITY SETUP (Weeks 2-6)

- [ ] Register LLC with your Secretary of State
- [ ] Get EIN from IRS (free, instant online)
- [ ] Open business bank account (separate from personal)
- [ ] Get business phone number (Google Voice works)
- [ ] Set up professional business email (<name@yourdomain.com>)
- [ ] Create business website (even a single page works)
- [ ] Register with Dun & Bradstreet for DUNS number
- [ ] File for business licenses in your city/county

---

## PHASE 3: BUSINESS CREDIT BUILDING (Weeks 6-12)

- [ ] Apply for Net-30 vendor accounts:
  - [ ] Uline
  - [ ] Grainger
  - [ ] Summa Office Supplies
  - [ ] Quill
  - [ ] Strategic Network Solutions
- [ ] Make small purchases and pay within terms
- [ ] Monitor Dun & Bradstreet PAYDEX score
- [ ] Target: 5+ tradelines reporting
- [ ] Apply for business credit cards:
  - [ ] Chase Ink Business
  - [ ] American Express Blue Business
  - [ ] Capital One Spark

---

## PHASE 4: FUNDING APPLICATION (Weeks 12-16)

- [ ] Prepare business plan (1-2 pages)
- [ ] Gather required documentation:
  - [ ] 2 years personal tax returns
  - [ ] 6 months business bank statements
  - [ ] Business license copies
  - [ ] EIN letter
  - [ ] Articles of Organization
- [ ] Calculate debt-to-income ratio
- [ ] Apply for SBA microloans ($500-$50,000)
- [ ] Apply for business line of credit
- [ ] Explore equipment financing (if applicable)

---

## PHASE 5: SCALING (Months 4-12)

- [ ] Maintain perfect payment history on all accounts
- [ ] Increase credit limits every 6 months (call and ask)
- [ ] Reinvest funding into revenue-generating assets
- [ ] Track ROI on every funded initiative
- [ ] Build relationship with local bank/CU loan officer
- [ ] Apply for larger SBA loans ($50K-$500K) at 6-12 months

---

## DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS CHEAT SHEET

| Lender Type | Min Credit Score | Min Revenue | Time in Business |
|------------|-----------------|-------------|-----------------|
| SBA Microloan | 620 | N/A | 0 months |
| SBA 7(a) | 680 | $100K+/yr | 2 years |
| Business LOC | 650 | $50K+/yr | 6 months |
| Equipment Financing | 600 | $50K+/yr | 1 year |
| Revenue-Based | 550 | $10K+/mo | 6 months |

---

## RED FLAGS THAT KILL APPLICATIONS

1. ❌ Personal and business finances mixed
2. ❌ Inconsistent business name across documents
3. ❌ No business bank account
4. ❌ Recent credit inquiries (>6 in 6 months)
5. ❌ Outstanding tax liens or judgments
6. ❌ No business website or online presence
