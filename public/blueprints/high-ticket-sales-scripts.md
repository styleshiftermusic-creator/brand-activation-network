## SCRIPT 1: THE WEBINAR CLOSE

### Opening (First 5 Minutes)

"Before we start, I want to be upfront with you. At the end of this training, I'm going to offer you the chance to work with me directly. If that's not for you, no problem — the training alone will give you massive value. But if you ARE someone who wants to accelerate your results, stay until the very end because I've got something special."

### The 3-Secret Framework (Minutes 5-50)

- **Secret 1:** [Teach your first major insight — give real value]
- **Secret 2:** [Teach your second insight — address a common mistake]
- **Secret 3:** [Teach your third insight — show the complete picture]

### The Transition (Minute 50)

"So now you know the WHAT. You know WHAT to do. But there's a difference between knowing what to do and having someone guide you through HOW to do it, step by step, with accountability. That's where [Your Program Name] comes in."

### The Stack (Minutes 50-60)

"Here's everything you get:

- [Module/Component 1] — Value: $X
- [Module/Component 2] — Value: $X
- [Module/Component 3] — Value: $X
- [Bonus 1] — Value: $X
- [Bonus 2] — Value: $X

Total value: $XX,XXX

But you're not paying that. Not even close.

The investment for [Program Name] is just $[Price]."

## SCRIPT 2: THE STRATEGY CALL (Sales Call)

### Phase 1: Build Rapport (5 min)

"Hey [Name], great to meet you. Before we dive in, tell me — what made you sign up for this call today? What was the thing that clicked?"

*(Let them talk. Listen. Mirror their language.)*

### Phase 2: Identify Pain (10 min)

"Walk me through where your [business/brand/finances] are right now."

*(Then ask these deepening questions:)*

- "How long have you been dealing with this?"
- "What have you tried so far?"
- "Why do you think those things didn't work?"
- "On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is it for you to fix this?"

### Phase 3: Paint the Vision (5 min)

"If we could wave a magic wand and 12 months from now everything was working exactly how you want — what does that look like?"

*(Let them describe their dream outcome in detail. Take notes.)*

### Phase 4: Present the Solution (10 min)

"Based on everything you've told me, here's exactly how I'd help you get from [current state] to [desired state]:

Step 1: [First milestone]
Step 2: [Second milestone]
Step 3: [Third milestone]

This is the exact process that helped [client name] achieve [specific result]."

### Phase 5: Handle the Close (10 min)

"The investment for the program is $[Price]. We can do [payment plan option] or [full pay with discount]. Which works best for you?"

*(Then STOP TALKING. Let them respond.)*

## SCRIPT 3: OBJECTION HANDLERS

### "I need to think about it"

"I totally get that. Can I ask — what specifically do you need to think about? Is it the price, the program, or something else?"

*(Address their specific concern, then:)*

"Here's what I've found — the people who succeed are the ones who decide quickly and act. The ones who 'think about it' usually end up in the same place 6 months from now. Which person do you want to be?"

### "I can't afford it"

"I hear you. Let me ask you this — can you afford NOT to? What's it costing you every month to stay where you are?"

*(Let them calculate the cost of inaction.)*

"We have a payment plan of $[amount]/month. Think of it as an investment that pays for itself if you [specific ROI they'd get]."

### "I need to ask my spouse"

"Of course, that makes sense. Would it help if I jumped on a quick call with both of you? That way I can answer any questions they might have too."

### "I've been burned before"

"I'm sorry to hear that. What happened?"

*(Listen to their story, then:)*

"That's exactly why I do things differently. [Explain your guarantee/support structure]. You're not alone in this — I'm with you every step."

### "Is there a guarantee?"

"Yes. If you show up, do the work, and implement what I teach, and you don't see [specific result] in [timeframe], I'll [your guarantee — refund, extra coaching, etc.]."
